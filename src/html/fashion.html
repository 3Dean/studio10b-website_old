Fashion Show Runway

Project Overview:

The Fashion Show Runway project was conceived as an innovative space to showcase 3D avatar models. Designed to blend style with digital artistry, this environment serves as a virtual stage for fashion presentation.

Project Objective:

Our aim was to create an engaging and spacious virtual environment, accommodating up to 25 guests. This space was specifically designed for viewing avatars in a fashion show setting, offering a unique blend of fashion and technology.

Creative Process:

Inspired by various fashion show designs, I envisioned a sleek white room adorned with dynamic shapes to set the perfect backdrop for the runway. Utilizing Blender, I meticulously crafted the models and materials to capture the essence of a high-fashion atmosphere. These elements were then exported as GLBs and seamlessly integrated into Mozilla Hubs, creating an immersive and stylish virtual runway experience.
