Open Web Sandbox Theater

Project Overview:

As the first NEAR in-house recruitment service for top-tier developers, Open Web Sandbox entrusted us with a unique project. Our mission was to design a 3D virtual theater, drawing inspiration from the iconic Bolshoi Theatre in Moscow. This immersive space is tailored to facilitate training, education, and resource provision for partners engaged in developing NEAR protocol projects.

Project Objective:

We set out to capture the grandeur and inspirational essence of the Bolshoi Theatre, transforming it into a digital arena for presentations and collaborative endeavors within the NEAR community.

Design Approach:

Informed by extensive research on the Bolshoi Theatre, I commenced with detailed sketches to capture its architectural majesty. The modeling and texturing of the theater were meticulously executed using 3D Coat, Photoshop, and Blender, ensuring an authentic and captivating 3D experience that resonates with the splendor of the original landmark.
