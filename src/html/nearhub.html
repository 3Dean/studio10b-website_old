NEARHUB

Project Overview:

Commissioned by NEARhub, we were tasked with crafting a branded virtual space for NearHub. This innovative environment was designed to showcase special projects, enriched with videos and links for guest interaction.

Project Objective:

The primary goal of this initiative was to amplify awareness of the pioneering projects associated with NEAR cryptocurrency. We aimed to create an interactive and educational platform, fostering engagement and discovery.

Design Process:

In the creation of this virtual space, I utilized Blender for modeling, focusing on material-based aesthetics to achieve a sleek and crisp environment. The space is further brought to life with a particle generator in Mozilla Hubs, simulating the serene effect of falling leaves, adding a dynamic and immersive element to the experience.
